Agora's SD-RTN vs the Public Internet - Video Calling Quality Comparison



Test: This page compares call quality over Agora's network (SD-RTN) with Peer to Peer (p2p) over the public internet.
Method: Two video calls are established with a remote session over both SD-RTN and p2p.
The locally generated clock video is sent over both calls and echoed back by the remote user.
The clocks are read using OCR and metrics are obtained for connection time, latency, jitter, bitrate, framerate volatility and packet loss.
These stats cover the full path to the remote computer and back.
